About

I'm a full-stack developer who treats the browser as a canvas — equally comfortable wiring up a PostgreSQL schema as I am tuning a WebGL shader.

Over the past several years I've worked across product teams building dashboards, marketing sites, and real-time tools, always pushing for interfaces that respond to the person using them — not just static pages. I care about performance and accessibility as much as visual polish; a beautiful animation that jams on a mid-range phone isn't a win.

When I'm not shipping, I'm usually reverse-engineering some effect I saw on a site I admired, or contributing small fixes to open-source tools I rely on.
